About IEngage Track

IEngage Track (Industry Engagement) bridges students, professionals and future entrepreneurs with the industry by making them ready for the future. Sharpen your skills, unlock your potential, and witness innovation come alive. This track is all set to take you to the next level of product and startup mastery by providing training on foundational aspects from the Basics level of Cutting-Edge Technologies. IEngage Track has a key focus on Career Development, Industrial Skills Development and Startup/Entrepreneurship Development with our idea “New Age IEngage”.

Vision & Mission

Vision

By partnering with industry experts, YESIST12 unlocks exclusive career development opportunities, in-demand skill development workshops, and entrepreneurial mentorship for participants, empowering them to thrive in their chosen fields.

Mission

Creating pathways for pre-university and university students to collaborate with industry professionals on projects that address critical social challenges. Unleashing the power of social responsibility through community engagement. We empower young minds to become tech-powered changemakers for a brighter tomorrow.

Pillars

Career Building

We empower young professionals to build a strong foundation for their careers by equipping them with in-demand technical skills and allow them to demonstrate with innovations.

Industry Skills Development

Embrace continuous learning and develop industry knowledge to stay relevant and competitive.

Start-up/ Entrepreneurship Development

IEngage provides right guidance with the three stages of startup namely early-stage, venture-funded (growth) stage and late stage. IEngage unlocks your entrepreneurial potential and fuels your confidence for success.

Objective:Our Pathway

Mentor Mentee Program

Mentor-Mentee Program is a structured learning and development program that pairs experienced individuals (mentors) with less experienced individuals (mentees) for guidance, support, and knowledge transfer. We will be finding the right mentors who can take YESIST12 Participants as their mentees and guide them through the solution building journey. The best mentor-mentee pair will be identified and rewarded.

FAQs | IEngage Track

1. What does IEngage Track focus on?
We focus on taking innovators and participants to the next level of product and startup building.
2. What benefits will participants get from IEngage Track?
They receive mentorship and training from industry experts to improve their startup and professional journey.
3. How is IEngage Track different from other IEEE YESIST12 tracks?
IEngage Track provides participants with the guidance and knowledge to turn their product ideas into reality, setting it apart from other tracks.
4. Will there be any call for abstracts for IEngage Track?
No, IEngage Track focuses on guiding participants directly through workshops, mentorship, and interactive sessions.
5. Who can participate in IEngage Track?
Students, professionals, innovators, and teams passionate about sustainable development and innovation.
6. How are projects evaluated?
Projects are evaluated on creativity, relevance to SDGs, scalability, and deliverability.
7. How to register for IEngage Track sessions?
Registration will be open via Google Forms or links on the official website.
8. Will there be prizes or awards?
No monetary prizes, but participants gain valuable knowledge from keynote speakers and industry experts.
9. What is the Mentor-Mentee Program?
A dedicated program connecting industry professionals with participants to mentor them in building solutions effectively.
